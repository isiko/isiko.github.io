Flying FPV

First-person view (FPV for short) drones, sometimes called racing drones, are the other kind of drones that most people have never heard of. They differ in almost all respects from the “normal” camera drones known from manufacturers like DJI or Parrot. While the Parrot drone takes care of most of the flight control, here the pilot has to take care of the stabilization of the drone. What seems like a big disadvantage at first glance is actually quite an advantage for FPV drones: With the extended level of control from the pilot, the pilot also has more options of what he can do with the aircraft....
